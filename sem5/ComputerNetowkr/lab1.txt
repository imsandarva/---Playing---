# Lab 1: Peer-to-Peer Network in Cisco Packet Tracer
## Step-by-Step Manual

---

## OBJECTIVE

- Learn to use Cisco Packet Tracer
- Connect two PCs directly (peer-to-peer)
- Test connectivity

---

## WHAT IS PEER-TO-PEER (P2P)?

Two computers connected directly without a switch or router.
Both can share files and communicate equally.

---

## STEP 1: OPEN CISCO PACKET TRACER

Launch the application on your Ubuntu system.

---

## STEP 2: ADD TWO PCs

1. Click **End Devices** icon (computer icon at bottom)
2. Click **PC**
3. Drag **2 PCs** onto the workspace
4. They'll be named PC0 and PC1

---

## STEP 3: CONNECT THE PCs

1. Click **Connections** icon (lightning bolt)
2. Select **Copper Cross-Over** cable (dotted line)
3. Click **PC0** → select **FastEthernet0**
4. Click **PC1** → select **FastEthernet0**
5. Wait for orange dots to turn **green** (~30 seconds)

---

## STEP 4: CONFIGURE PC0 IP ADDRESS

1. Click **PC0**
2. Click **Desktop** tab
3. Click **IP Configuration**
4. Enter:
   - IP Address: **192.168.1.1**
   - Subnet Mask: **255.255.255.0**
5. Close window

---

## STEP 5: CONFIGURE PC1 IP ADDRESS

1. Click **PC1**
2. Click **Desktop** tab
3. Click **IP Configuration**
4. Enter:
   - IP Address: **192.168.1.2**
   - Subnet Mask: **255.255.255.0**
5. Close window

---

## STEP 6: TEST CONNECTIVITY

### From PC0:

1. Click **PC0** → **Desktop** → **Command Prompt**
2. Type: `ping 192.168.1.2`
3. Press Enter

**Expected:** 4 successful replies ✓

### From PC1:

1. Click **PC1** → **Desktop** → **Command Prompt**
2. Type: `ping 192.168.1.1`
3. Press Enter

**Expected:** 4 successful replies ✓

---

## SCREENSHOTS NEEDED

1. Network topology (2 PCs connected)
2. PC0 IP configuration
3. PC1 IP configuration
4. Successful ping from PC0 to PC1

---

## TROUBLESHOOTING

**If ping fails:**
- Wait 30-60 seconds (connection needs time)
- Check IPs are in same network (192.168.1.x)
- Verify cable shows green dots
- Check you used Cross-Over cable

---

## CONCLUSION

Successfully connected two PCs directly in a peer-to-peer network. 
Configured IP addresses and verified connectivity using ping command.
